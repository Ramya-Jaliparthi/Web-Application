Add Your Race, Ethnicity, and Language

At Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts, we are committed to being part of a health care system that provides safe, timely, effective, affordable, patient-centered care for all. But research shows that there are measurable differences in the health care provided to people in Massachusetts based on their race, ethnicity, or primary language. To better understand these differences, and expand access to care, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts would like to ask you to share information about your race, ethnicity, and language. Sharing this information is completely voluntary, and we will hold it in the strictest confidence. To better understand differences in access to and use of health care, selected state health organizations will receive information that is de-identified, meaning your personal identity is removed from the answers.
