Insurance Terms

Overall Deductible

The dollar amount that a member must pay for health care services before a health plan will cover eligible services.

For example, if member's deductible is $500, the member will pay that amount, out-of-pocket, before the health plan will cover eligible services.

Co-Pay

A co-pay is a fixed amount you pay for a health care service, usually when you receive the service. The amount can vary by the type of service. You may also have a co-pay when you get a prescription filled.
