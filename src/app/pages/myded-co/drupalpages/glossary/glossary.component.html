Deductible & Co-Insurance Glossary

Overall Deductible The dollar amount that a member must pay for medical services before a health plan will cover eligible services. Effective Date The date on which coverage under a health benefits plan begins. Exceptions & Exclusions Applies to Group 2 and Group 3 services combined. It does not apply to any enhanced benefits. Out-of-Pocket Maximum The maximum amount a plan will pay for covered services during a plan year.
